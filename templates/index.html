Welcome to BurritoBuddy! This is a database of burritos in San Diego, California. The app also shows you a given burrito's seven closest buddies, or the burritos that are most similar to it based on it's price, fillings, and reviews. Just two steps to hot, delicious goodness:

  1. Select a Burrito you've previously enjoyed (from the dropdown menu)
  2. Click "Submit"
  3. Wait patiently while we figure out it's buddies
  4. Check the list
  5. Explore San Diego and enjoy our suggestions
  6. Pick your favorite burrito from the list and start again untill you've tried them all!
  7. Suggested: Take a periodic break, your cholesterol will thank you

How did I make this?

I started by searching for a dataset of individual burritos with supporting data. Luckily kaggle had this dataset, which suited nicely, despite the limitation to burritos in San Diego. I then used K-Nearest Neighbors to cluster the burritos by price and ingredients (represented as a one hot encoded 1d vector). Clusters of 7 burritos are then displayed. The webapp runs on Flask for routing and forms, Pandas to manipulate data, and SciKit-Learn for finding clusters.

Why did I make this?

Two of my great passions in life are Data Science and Mexican Cuisine. The Bay Area's reputation for quality taqueria's was a signifiant motivator for my move out west. It was only natural that I've blended the two interests here.

I hope it serves you well next time you're in San Diego, and stay tuned for expansions into Northern California.
